About Us

The Ming's Diner Restaurant was originally opened in Banglore, on September 29, 1999.
Currently there are 150 franchise in India and 35 franchise across the globe.
Main branch of the famous Ming's Diner is in Banglore India.

The subtle ambience of the Ming's Diner restaurant plays a significant role in shaping our customer's dining experience.
And the vast variety of cuisine, service and environment together gives you a pleasant vibes.

Meals are generally served and eaten on the premises, but Ming's Diner restaurants also offer take-out and food delivery services. Restaurants vary greatly in appearance and offerings
